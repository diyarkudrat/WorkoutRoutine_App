Pre-Workout and Post-Workout Exercises for an Arms Workout

Overview

  1. Tricep Extensions
  2. Biceps Warm Up Curl
  3. Forearm Curl
  4. Overhead Tricep Stretch
  5. Sidearm Raises

1. Pre-Workout

Tricep Extensions

Equipment Needed: One 5 lb dumbell

Repetition: 2 sets of 15 reps

Step-by-Step:

  • Grip the dumbell with both hands. Start the weight above your head.
  • Bend the elbows back, lowering the weight behind your head.
  • Slowly return to the starting position and repeat.


Bicep Curl Warm Up

Equipment Needed: 2 10 lb dumbells

Repetition: 2 sets of 15 reps

Step-by-Step:

  • Hold a dumbell with each hand.
  • Start with your arms curled in, and dumbells at the shoulder height.
  • Lower your arms until fully extended and bring them back to the starting postion.


2. Post-Workout

Overhead Tricep Stretch

Equipment Needed: None

Repetition: 45 seconds for each arm

Step-by-Step:

  • Raise your left elbow and reach toward your upper back. Elbow should be toward the ceiling.
  • Pull your left elbow with your right hand gently and slowly until you feel a stretch in your muscle. Hold that postion for 45 seconds
  • Repeat for the other arm.


Sidearm Raises

Equipment Needed:

  • One 5 lb dumbell
  • If too heavy, don't use a dumbell

    • Repetition: 2 sets of 15 reps for each arm.(4 sets total)

    Step-by-Step:

    • You can do this sitting down or standing up.
    • grip the dumbell and place your arm out to your side.
    • Lift the dumbell slowly with a slight bend in your elbow.
    • Make sure the movement is ocurring in your arm.
