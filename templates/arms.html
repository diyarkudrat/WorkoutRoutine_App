1. Warmup

Internal Rotation

Equipment Needed: One 5 lb weighted plate

Repetition: 2 sets of 10 reps for each arm

Step-by-Step:

  • Hold onto the 5lb plate with your elbow bent at 90 degrees. Your hand should be in front of your body.
  • Keep your elbow as tight as possible to your body and bring the arm across the body
  • Slowly return to the starting position and repeat
