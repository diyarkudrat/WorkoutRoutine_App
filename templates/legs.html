Pre-Workout

Foam Roller

Equipment Needed: Foam Roller

Repetition: 30 seconds each leg

Step-by-Step:

  • Place foam roller under your hamstrings. Elevate your body with your hands.
  • Move your body back and forth, allowing your body to apply pressure on the roller. This gets rid of any knots in your hamstrings and loosens them.
  • Do the same steps as above, but flip your body over and place the roller under your quadriceps.


Stationary Bike

Equipment Needed: Stationary Bike

Repetition: 3-4 minutes

Description:

  • Bike on a nice low level to warm up your legs and get the blood flowing.
