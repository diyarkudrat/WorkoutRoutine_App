1. Warmup

Internal Rotation

Equipment Needed: One 5 lb weighted plate

Repetition: 2 sets of 10 reps for each arm

Step-by-Step:

  • Hold onto the 5lb plate with your elbow bent at 90 degrees. Your hand should be in front of your body.
  • Keep your elbow as tight as possible to your body and bring the arm across the body
  • Slowly return to the starting position and repeat


External Rotation With Arm Abducted 90Â°

Equipment Needed: One 5 lb weighted plate

Repetition: 2 sets of 10 for each arm

Step-by-Step:

  • Hold onto 5lb plate with your elbow bent at 90 degrees. Your hand should be facing upwards.
  • Keeping your shoulder and elbow level, slowly lower your hand until it is in line with your head.
  • Slowly return to the starting position and repeat


Arm Circles

Equipment needed: One 5 lb weighted plate(optional)

Repetition: 2 sets of 10 reps. 5 reps clockwise. 5 reps counter-clockwise

Step-by-Step:

  • Hold onto 5lb plate(optional). Arms are extended and elbows are locked.
  • Slowly move arms in a circular motion keeping the elbows locked.
  • Repeat.

2. Incline Dumbell Bench Press

Equipment Needed: Two Dumbells. *REMEMBER* Weight does NOT matter. Start off with light weight and work your way up in weight.

Repetition: 5 sets of 10 reps (I know this is a lot of reps. I believe in you. You can do it)

Description: To bring the weight up, put the dumbells on your lap. By lifting your knees up, use that momentum to bring up the weight. The weight will end up by your shoulders and that's when you push up the weight. For each rep, bring the weight down until the end of the dumbell is a little bit above the level of your chest, and press up. Make sure the weight is controlled when you bring it up and down.


3. Machine Flys

Repetition: 4 sets of 10 reps

Description: Make sure your elbows aren't locked out. There should be a slight bent to your arms. Make sure your wrists are locked. Bring your hands together and bring them back to the starting position. Make sure that you don't overextend or go too fast or else you will tear a muscle and mess up your chest.


4. Machine Press

Repetition: 4 sets of 15 reps

Description: Don't go too heavy with the weight or else you will burn out quickly. Don't move the weight really fast. Make sure it's controlled and at a resonable speed so you really work those chest muscles.

5. Pushdowns

Repetition: 4 sets of 15 reps

Description: Standing up,hold onto the platform and bring the shoulder blades together and press down.


6. Rehab Exercise

Equipment Needed: A pole or the edge of a wall

Repetition: 1 minute for each arm

Description: Grab onto the wall or pole with your right hand. Extend out your arm. Bring your left shoulder back and right shoulder forward slightly. You will feel a nice stretch in your right pectoral area. Repeat for the left pectoral.
