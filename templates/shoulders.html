Pre-Workout and Post-Workout Stretches for a Chest Workout

Overview

  1. Internal Rotation
  2. External Rotation With Arm Abducted 90°
  3. Arm Circles
  4. Rehab Exercise

Pre-Workout

Internal Rotation


Equipment Needed: One 5 lb weighted plate

Repetition: 2 sets of 10 reps for each arm

Step-by-Step:

  • Hold onto the 5lb plate with your elbow bent at 90 degrees. Your hand should be in front of your body.
  • Keep your elbow as tight as possible to your body and bring the arm across the body
  • Slowly return to the starting position and repeat


External Rotation With Arm Abducted 90°

Equipment Needed: One 5 lb weighted plate

Repetition: 2 sets of 10 for each arm

Step-by-Step:

  • Hold onto 5lb plate with your elbow bent at 90 degrees. Your hand should be facing upwards.
  • Keeping your shoulder and elbow level, slowly lower your hand until it is in line with your head.
  • Slowly return to the starting position and repeat


Arm Circles

Equipment needed: One 5 lb weighted plate(optional)

Repetition: 2 sets of 10 reps. 5 reps clockwise. 5 reps counter-clockwise

Step-by-Step:

  • Hold onto 5lb plate(optional). Arms are extended and elbows are locked.
  • Slowly move arms in a circular motion keeping the elbows locked.
  • Repeat.

Post-Workout

Equipment Needed: A pole or the edge of a wall

Repetition: 1 minute for each arm

Description: Grab onto the wall or pole with your right hand. Extend out your arm. Bring your left shoulder back and right shoulder forward slightly. You will feel a nice stretch in your right pectoral area. Repeat for the left pectoral.
