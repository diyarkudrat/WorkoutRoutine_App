Pre-Workout

Foam Roller Back Extension

Equipment Needed: Foam Roller

Repetition: 1 minute

Step-by-Step:

  • Sit on floor with knees bent and feet planted. Place foam roller just above lower back.
  • Lie back on roller and reach your arms up overhead.
  • Bend backwards gently over the roller, exhaling and pushing your arms back.
  • Come back up and move foam roller up your back a few inches.


Toe Touches

Equipment Needed: None

Repetition: 2 sets of 30 seconds

Step-by-Step:

  • Stand with feet together and knees locked.
  • Slowly bend down with your hands toward the floor. Make sure your arms are loose. Take away all pressure from your arms.


2. Post-Workout

Cat Stretch

Equipment Needed: None

Repetition: 10 reps

Step-by-Step:

  • Get on your hands and knees
  • Round your back and breathe out through your mouth
  • Arch your back and inhale through your nose
  • Repeat.


Knee to Chest

Equipment Needed: None

Repetition: 30 seconds for each leg

Step-by-Step:

  • lie on your back.
  • Use both hands to pull one knee in towrad chest.
  • Hold knee for 30 seconds, keeping the abdominals tight.
  • Repeat with opposite leg.
